Woodworking Planes

Explore the world of woodworking planes

Overview

Woodworking planes are tools used for shaping wood. They are used to flatten, reduce the thickness of, and impart a smooth surface to a rough piece of lumber. As an indispensable part of the woodworker's tool chest, planes are used for various tasks including smoothing, shaping, and leveling wood surfaces.
